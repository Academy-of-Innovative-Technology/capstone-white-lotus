Who we are -

F1 is a nonprofit organization built on a simple but powerful idea: everyone deserves a fair starting point. The name “F1” stands for “First Step Forward,” reflecting the company’s mission to help people experiencing homelessness regain stability and independence, one step at a time.

At its core, F1 focuses on practical, immediate support. The organization operates mobile outreach units that travel through cities, providing essentials like meals, clean clothing, hygiene kits, and access to basic medical care. But F1 goes beyond short-term relief. Its real impact comes from its long-term programs designed to break the cycle of homelessness.

What sets F1 apart is its emphasis on dignity and personalization. Instead of a one-size-fits-all model, each person works with a case coordinator to create a tailored plan based on their needs—whether that involves mental health services, addiction recovery, or family reunification.

F1 also invests in prevention. By working with at-risk communities, offering rental assistance, and providing financial literacy workshops, the organization aims to stop homelessness before it begins. Ultimately, F1 isn’t just about helping people survive—it’s about helping them rebuild their lives with confidence and purpose.
